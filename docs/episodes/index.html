Prelude: The Beginning Is The End Is The Beginning

The one with all the hopping about in time, the introductions and the fight on the train.

  • Session 0.1, in which the Cabal-to-be face off with a rogue Moros on the late train to Edinburgh, a Ghost is bisected by an enchanted door and we see Cobalt and Samael’s Awakenings.
  • Session 0.2, in which Cobalt is recruited to the Free Council by Cognos, the Cabal-to-be meet for the first time and we see Persephone’s Awakening.
  • Session 0.3, in which the Consilium is introduced, Cognos leaves and Chayot is killed.

Story One: Touched

The one where the Cabal form officially, the other Mages are introduced properly and what seems to be a quiet investigation into a ghost turns into an Astral journey into the Oneiros of a soul-damaged Proximus.

  • Session 1.1, in which the Auric Horizon is named, Bede is shady and Cobalt meets Symmetry.
  • Session 1.2, in which the Ghost turns out to not be a Ghost after all, and we see the Farthest Legion’s Sanctum.
  • Session 1.3, in which the Cabal and Cicero plunge into the depths of Dominic’s Oneiros and are forced to make a harsh decision.

Story Two: Welcome To The Jungle

The one where the Cabal go to Brazil to join an Archaeomancer expedition, and end up fighting an ancient magical being and a group of local Seers of the Throne.

  • Session 2.1, in which the Cabal go to Rio and meet the rest of the team. And Cognos makes a Christmas appearance.
  • Session 2.2, in which there’s a high-stakes game of volleyball and the Expedition’s members capture a Seer. With sleep-disturbing consequences for Cobalt.
  • Session 2.3, in which the Expedition set off to Acre province, Samael and Duma clash and the Seers ambush the convoy.
  • Session 2.4, in which the Atlantean temple is reached and the Expedition come under attack from both sides.
  • Session 2.5, in which Persephone and Orchid flee the wreckage, Samael and Duma try to understand the temple’s interior and Cobalt and Ashlar try to survive.

Story Three: The Hounds Of Winter

The one with the Barghests, the Werewolf and the Mysterium screwing over the Free Council. Also with the introduction of Kosciej, and the revelation of just what’s going on inside Persephone’s head.

  • Session 3.1, in which Persephone enters her Oneiros and finds Kosciej lurking there, Excalibur tries to watch a hockey game and the others find a frozen corpse.
  • Session 3.2, in which the gang break into a Police Station and Persephone explains Kosciej to Cobalt before having a huge fight with Samael.
  • Session 3.3, in which Cobalt learns to Duel, the investigation continues and the Hounds are found in a most unlikely place.
  • Session 3.4, in which a delicate situation becomes even more so and Persephone goes in search of the Parliament of the Needle
  • Session 3.5, in which the real Kosciej is contacted and the Mysterium bite off more than they can chew, to the detriment of Symmetry’s career.

Story Four: When You Were Young

The one where the Cabal go to London for a holiday and end up getting deeper into the conspiracy, fighting off fear spirits and a particularly single-minded Echo Walker while trying to not get arrested by the paranoid Guardian of the Veil regime.

  • Session 4.1, in which Ashlar, Pandora and Rodriguez return to the Chronicle and Crystal Skulls are examined.
  • Session 4.2, in which Samael examines the Sophiad and learns about the Ankh and Cobalt and Persephone find Hadrian.
  • Session 4.3, in which the Cabal are attacked by fear Spirits and figure out what Chayot was up to last summer.
  • Session 4.4, in which Rodriguez wins his Duel, Pandora and Samael become too close for Persephone’s comfort and Astral Journies are made.
  • Session 4.5, in which a soul-damaged Samael refuses to face off against Arc, leaving Cobalt to do it instead.

Story Five: Monster

The one where the Cabal stumble across the Lambton Worm, the Consilium is attacked from within by a small group determined to see it in civil war and Arc continues his one-man campaign.

  • Session 5.1, in which Cobalt picks up his mail and discovers an ancient monster out of folklore living under Tynemouth Priory.
  • Session 5.2, in which the Cabal’s attempts to heal the Consilium coincide with someone else’s much more concerted attempts to crack it like an egg.
  • Session 5.3, in which the investigation into the break-ins gathers pace like a rolling boulder, until the traitors are brought down by the angry mob and the Cabal realise just how bad things are looking for Cat.
  • Session 5.4, in which the Cabal fight off the Lambton Worm and Samael goes to see Key.
  • Session 5.5, in which the Consilium decides on many matters, Arc presents a proposed deal and one of the Cabal’s allies is banished.

Story Six: Butterflies and Hurricanes

the one which is a tour of the Astral Realms as the Cabal seek Hades’ Oneiros, the Temenos Realm of the Sphinx and Sophia’s Chantry on the far shores of the Fallen World. Mordred is a Dick, and Kosciej makes his move.

  • Session 6.1, in which the Cabal get their marching Orders, and D!K gets the chance to stage the Council of Elrond he’s always wanted to stage.
  • Session 6.2, in which the Cabal dig through Persephone and Hades’ Oneiroi, and many revelations about what went down in London are made.
  • Session 6.3, in which the Cabal ascend to the Temenos and visit the Realms of many Legacies, stopping to save Natalie from the Realm of the Salem Witch Hunts along the way.
  • Session 6.4, in which the Anima Mundi is climbed, Symmetry meets an Owl and Mordred is a dick.
  • Session 6.5, in which Newcastle erupts into Civil War and Samael meets an Archmage.

Story Seven: Children of the Revolution

The one in which the Cabal go to Paris for a Convocation, and everyone frames everyone else for murder.

  • Session 7.1, in which the Cabal meet a reasonable and well-respected Heirarch for a change, and murder is committed.
  • Session 7.2, in which Kosciej fakes faking evidence against himself and gets away with it.

Story Eight: Island of Souls

GENCON 2009: The one in which Excalibur and Rod make new friends and form a Cabal of their own, while fighting Seers in Wales

  • Session 8

Story Nine: What I’ve Done

The one in which the Cabal excavate the lost city of Cxaxa Querephas, face three Pylons of Seers and a Minotaur and Cxaxa completes her long-deferred ressurection with Samael’s help

  • Session 9.1, in which the Cabal meet up with their Archaeomantic chums, stories are swapped and Cities are found.
  • Session 9.2, in which the Cabal fight Seers, traverse the Labyrinth and Samael makes a hard choice.

Story Ten: Uprising

The one in which Cobalt and Persephone go to Manchester to head the Nameless off Politically, and Samael has a hit put out on him by the Exarchs.

  • Session 10

Interlude Fiction: Where Does It Go?

  • Part One: Mind The Gap, in which Pandora and Ashlar have a conversation
  • Part Two: Aftermath, in which the Boudicca has had enough

  • Part Four: The First Sign of Madness, in which Djehuty and Brianna spar, and Kosciej talks to D!Persephone

Story Eleven: Deeper Underground

The one in which the Cabal face off against the Echo Walkers, the Seers of the Throne and the Lambton Worm

  • Session 11.1, in which Bede is cagey, the Seers step up their attacks and the Consilium battle the Echo Walkers at Penshaw Monument
  • Session 11.2, in which Kosciej makes veiled threats, and Cobalt institutes a new tea-kitty policy
  • Session 11.3, in which the Consilium storm the Seer of the Throne sanctum and Lankin is killed.
  • Session 11.4, in which the Lambton Worm is destroyed, and the Cabal put a risky plan into motion.
